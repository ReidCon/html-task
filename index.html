Why HTML?

HTML (Following Paragraph is Left Aligned)

HTML is responsible of giving a semantic meaning for your content. For example :

  1. Here is a <h5> title
  2. Followed by a <p>paragraph </p>
  3. Then there’s a footer [etc].
Web browsers read it to display your content according to their importance (a title is bigger than a text, a <strong> "strong"</strong> text is more important than a single text, etc). <tt>
